# Regeular Expression - Regex Collection

### Currency format with comma

Expression: 
`^(\d{1,3})(,\d{1,3})*(\.\d{1,})?$`

Sample Inputs: 

Input:
`123                   `
`1,111                 `
`11,234                `
`234,678               `
`1,111,444             `
`6666,7777,888         `
`777777777777777777.77 `
`1.2                   `
`2.55                  `
